Features

Everything a consortium needs

From budget setup to final reporting — Alloc8 handles the complexity of multi-partner EU projects so you can focus on the research.

📦

Work Package Management

Define WPs with start and end months, add tasks, and assign lead partners. The Gantt chart updates automatically as you build.

💶

Automated Budget Calculation

Person-months × monthly rate = direct costs. Indirect costs applied automatically. Budget summaries update in real-time as you enter data.

🤖

AI Skill Matching

Partners add their URLs — LinkedIn, publications, GitHub. AI scrapes and analyses them against your Work Packages to produce compatibility scores.

📊

Partner Progress Tracking

Each partner marks task cells as Not Started, In Progress, or Completed. All partners see each other's progress on the shared Gantt.

📄

One-Click PDF Report

Generate a full landscape A4 report with charts, Gantt timeline, budget tables, skill radar charts, and dependency networks — instantly, client-side.

📧

Invite-Based Partner Access

Coordinators add partners and send login credentials by email. Partners log straight in — no sign-up process, no friction.
